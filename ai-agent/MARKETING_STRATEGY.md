## 1. Campaign Architecture: "Lead Gen Alpha"
**Goal:** Maximize MQLs (Marketing Qualified Leads) & Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR).

### **Budget Allocation ($X/mo)**
- **Facebook/Instagram (60%):** Cold traffic, Lookalike audiences.
- **Google Ads (30%):** High-intent search ("Cloud hosting", "AI marketing tools").
- **Retargeting (10%):** Site visitors who didn't convert (Cart Abandonment).

### **Target Audience Segments**
1.  **"The Solopreneur":** Needs easy hosting + marketing content (Pain point: No time).
2.  **"The Agency Owner":** Needs white-label solutions (Pain point: Scalability).
3.  **"The E-com Seller":** Needs traffic for products (Pain point: CAC too high).

## 2. Tracking & Analytics Setup
**Infrastructure:**
- **GA4:** Event tracking (scroll_90, view_pricing, begin_checkout).
- **GTM:** Triggers for 'Form Submit' and 'WhatsApp Click'.
- **Hotjar:** Heatmaps to analyze bounce rate (<40% goal).

**UTM Schema:**
`?utm_source=[fb|google]&utm_medium=[cpc|organic]&utm_campaign=[lead_gen_alpha]&utm_content=[video_v1|carousel_v2]`

**Attribution Model:** Time-Decay (Give credit to touchpoints closer to conversion).

## 3. Creative Strategy
**Ad Copy Frameworks:**
- **PAS:** Problem (No traffic) -> Agitation (Sales dropping) -> Solution (Hostamar AI).
- **AIDA:** Attention (Video hook) -> Interest (AI automation) -> Desire (10x leads) -> Action (Sign up).

**CTA Variations (A/B Testing):**
- Control: "Start Free Trial"
- Var A: "Get My Marketing Video"
- Var B: "Scale My Business"

## 4. Automation (Nurture Flows)
**Tool:** Silas (AI Agent) + SendGrid/ActiveCampaign.
1.  **Welcome Seq:** Immediate delivery of value (PDF/Video) -> Soft upsell.
2.  **Abandon Cart:** 1 hour delay -> "Did you forget your AI tools?" -> 10% discount.
3.  **Active User:** Usage tips -> "Refer a friend" (Viral loop).

## 5. KPI Dashboard & Formulas
**Metric targets:**
- **CTR:** > 5%
- **Page Speed:** > 90/100 (Mobile)
- **Form Rate:** +15% MoM

**Formulas:**
- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost):** `Total Ad Spend / New Paying Customers`
- **LTV (Lifetime Value):** `ARPU (Avg Revenue Per User) * Avg Customer Lifespan`
- **ROAS (Return on Ad Spend):** `Revenue from Ads / Cost of Ads`
